IDENTITY_LEDGER · APPEND-ONLY
R1 · WITNESSED

Bindings are written only by the daemon. An agent's opinion of its own name is never an input. ant whoami first, always.

R2 · NO COLLISIONS

One live binding per handle. Vacant → claimed instantly. Occupied → refused. Integrity, not permission.

R3 · LOUD & REVERSIBLE

Rebinds and role assignments are allowed, ledgered, owner-notified — and held for confirmation when the approval bit is on. Any mistake is one rebind from fixed.

R4 · APPEND-ONLY

Every claim, vacancy, rebind, refusal and post lands in the ledger with who/what/when/lineage. The audit trail is the product.

THE INVARIANT — every entity keeps at least one human in its owner chain, and the last human is irremovable. Nothing in ANT is ever unaccountable.
